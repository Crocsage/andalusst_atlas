Atlas Beta Changelog - Apr. 11

Welcome to the Andalusst Atlas! Your guide to the guilds and the many Pokémon teams within.

Here you'll be able to properly register your team(s) with a guild, so that the others know who they can call on in times of need.

You can login via DeviantArt and get started! Logging in is done through DA, who then says "Yep, they're <person>", we never see your password. All we get is your username and avatar. The full process of this involves openAuth, and though it will request permission to access your Stash, it is not used. Once you've been identified, the DA connection is broken and no Stash access remains. If I could remove the permission to access it I would, but I can't!

Once you have a team registered, others group members will be able to search by team name, guild, or Pokémon and find your team. Of course, you can also use this to find teams yourself, whether for cameos or just to find a cool new team worth following!

Your teams will also be able to keep track of their star coins here, and (soon enough!) be able to use them to purchase accessories and items. Making it much easier to track your money and inventory!

There's more to come and if you have any suggestions, or worse find any bugs, you are encouraged to send me an email.

Thank you!
- Dr. Dos
