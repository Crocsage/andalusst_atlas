Atlas Update - 2015-02-14

General

  • The search page now shows teams which haven't submitted a logbook in the past 120 days in grayscale to mark them as inactive. You can filter searches to not show these teams. Inactive teams are just marked, they will not be removed, and a team being marked inactive does not necessarily mean the team's creator is no longer active at all in the group.
  • The Pokémon Population stats page now shows counts of Pokémon on active teams, as well as the grand total. The listings can be sorted by species, active total, and grand total.

Teams

  • Teams will now show shiny X/Y Pokémon models if the teammate is marked a shiny

Atlas Update - 2014-08-10

General

Atlas Update - 2014-07-15

General

  • Your active team's merits/strikes should update when visiting any page that updates starcoins (manage teams, the index, the shop)

Teams

  • The hideous mismash of duplicated code that was the Team Creation page, Pokemon Creation page, Team Editing page, Pokemon Editing page, and Team Viewing page has been completely rewritten. It is now the team page. Which means adding new fields or fixing bugs will go a lot faster. It also looks way prettier.
  • Locks have been implemented for teams/pokemon! Right now, every team/pokemon already on the Atlas is locked. This means that certain fields can't be edited. You can't just change species or guild affiliation any longer. Since most people weren't doing this to begin with, you should be fine. If your locked data is incorrect, yell at an admin. They will then yell at me because they can't unlock teams/pokemon yet. Adding that is #1 priority and will be live by this weekend (it won't be difficult to write at all).
  • When you make a new team, or add a new Pokemon to an existing team, it will be unlocked for 24 hours, during this time you can edit all fields freely if you catch any mistakes or change your mind about something.
  • Some new fields have been added to teams/pokemon. Teams can now have an alternate app, which is worthwhile if you have a hybrid team with both apps completed. Stats for Pokemon have also been implemented. On all your locked pokemon. So they all have stats of 0. Ha ha.
  • On the manage team page, you'll see a "Team Actions" link. This is where misc team related things will go. Such as setting stats for Pokemon that are all 0's! Please use this to get the relevant data added! Keep in mind once it is set, it can't be changed!

Team Inventory

  • Rewards package contents that offered multiple copies of the same item now properly give those multiple copies. Admins have manually fixed these, but if your team has the wrong number of customization vouchers, let them know!
  • Evolution Slips are now functioning! Go to your team's inventory and select the item, then click the "Evolve Teammate" button. You'll be able to select who evolves, into what, and reassign their ability, moves, and stats.

Cut Features! (I am actually Electronic Arts)

  • Deleting pokemon/teams is also temporarily gone. This is the #2 priority and will be done shortly (possibly with admins adjusting locks if I'm feeling motivated).

Atlas Update - 2014-04-11

General:

  • The selected team's starcoin balance is updated when you visit the front page, manage team page, and shop page. This should be enough to prevent it from desyncing with your real balance.
  • Fixed the Nidoran male/female symbols and accented é in Flabébé not hsowing up properly in some spots.
  • If you try to access a page that requires login, you'll be redirected to the login page rather than booted to the front page.
  • Added a "Random" to the list of sort by options for team searches.
  • Guild logos are now higher quality images.

Team Inventory:

  • You no longer have to be logged in to view a team's inventory
  • Customized items have a pair of scissors above them to indicate the item has been modified. They also display a history of customizations and link to the relevant DA page for each customization.

Teams:

  • Teams now have merits and strikes in the backend. They aren't displayed or used anywhere yet.
  • Your selected team now has a dresser icon that links directly to the team's inventory.

Shopping:

  • Purchasing an item/customization now shows you what you purchased afterwards, and links to your inventory.
  • You can now use vouchers to buy accessories. You can select which voucher you use, or choose to hold onto it and pay with starcoins (if you can!) instead.

Customizations:

  • You can now use Chantelle's accessory customization service. It is listed in the shop and will bring you to the relevant page. Customizations require a customizable item, a DA link to the submitted customization (drawn or written), and a custom name. A custom description can also be given but is not mandatory. It is YOUR RESPOSIBILITY to correctly select how many features are being customized as this effects the price and cannot be automated. If you lie to buy something for less you [DATA EXPUNGED]. For the sake of consistency, please set this to the correct amount of alterations even if you're paying with a voucher and making it cost no starcoins.

Items:

  • Customizations can be done multiple times provided you have the money/vouchers. Take a look at team Starbound's scarf as an example.
  • Added new items: Accessory Customization Voucher, Rookie Explorer Voucher, Rookie Hunter Voucher, and Rookie Researcher Voucher. These are the non-cash rewards for mission one.
