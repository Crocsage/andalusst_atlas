The Andalusst Atlas and Your DeviantArt Account

Here's how it works:

  1. You click that Authorize button.
  2. DeviantArt sends the Atlas a token to give us access limited data about your account.
  3. We run a "WhoamI" request on your account and get back something like this:
    {
    "username": "dr-dos",
    "userid": "XXXXXXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXXXXXXXXXX",
    "symbol": "",
    "usericon": "http://a.deviantart.net/avatars/d/r/dr-dos.gif",
    "usericonurl": "http://a.deviantart.net/avatars/d/r/dr-dos.gif"
    }
  4. We pull your username and avatar from that, and know that it's you.
And that's all! At no point is your sta.sh touched, nor will it ever be. We're unable to prevent that Sta.sh access, but we can promise that it is not used.
